Products

Our Export Products

Green Mung

Fassil Import exports, a company based in Addis Ababa, specializes in exporting premium-quality green mung beans globally. Renowned for our commitment to sustainable farming practices, we play a key role in promoting Ethiopia's agricultural products on the international market.

Oil seeds and pulses

Fasil Import and Export is a prominent Ethiopian company engaged in the trade of oil seeds and pulses. Renowned for our commitment to quality, our company plays a pivotal role in facilitating the export of Ethiopian agricultural products, contributing to the global supply chain of oil seeds and pulses.

Spices

Fasil Import and Export also excels in the spice trade, showcasing Ethiopia's rich culinary diversity. Our range includes indigenous spices like berbere and mitmita, contributing to the global spice market with products known for their unique flavors and cultural significance.

Our Import Products

Generators

Fasil Import and Export proudly includes generators in our range of import products, providing reliable and efficient power solutions for various sectors in Ethiopia. Whether for industrial use or backup power, our imported generators meet high standards, contributing to the country's energy infrastructure.

Water Pumps

Fasil Import and Export extends its product range to include water pumps, offering efficient solutions for agricultural, industrial, and domestic water needs in Ethiopia. Our imported water pumps are designed to enhance water accessibility and contribute to sustainable development.

Milling Machine

Fasil Import and Export is a leading player in the Ethiopian market, offering advanced milling machinery as part of our diverse import portfolio. Our state-of-the-art equipment ensures efficiency in grain processing, supporting the agricultural sector and enhancing food production capabilities in Ethiopia.
